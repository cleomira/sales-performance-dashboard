# sales-performance-dashboard
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing retail sales performance across regions, products, and customer segments.

📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive retail sales performance dashboard built in Power BI to analyze business performance across regions, products, and customer segments. The dashboard consolidates sales data into a centralized reporting interface, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The report was designed to provide actionable insights into revenue performance, customer behavior, product performance, and geographic sales distribution through interactive visualizations and dynamic filtering.

---

🎯 Objectives
The primary objectives of this project are to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze regional and state-level sales trends
- Identify top-performing product categories and products
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Support business decision-making with data-driven insights

---

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI** — Data visualization and dashboard development
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** — Measures and calculated metrics
- Microsoft Excel** — Data source and preprocessing

📊 Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Overview
This page provides a high-level summary of business performance through:
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Sales YoY %
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Segment

 Key Insight
- Sales demonstrated a strong upward trend over time, reaching peak performance in November 2018, indicating sustained business growth.

2. Geographic Analysis
This section focuses on regional and state-level sales performance.

Visuals Included
- Sales by Region
- Sales by State
- Top 5 States by Sales

Key Insights
- The West region generated the highest sales revenue, closely followed by the East region.
- The South region recorded the lowest sales performance, indicating potential growth opportunities.

---

3. Product & Customer Analysis
This page analyzes product performance and customer behavior.

Visuals Included
- Top Products by Sales
- Bottom Products by Sales
- Sales by Category
- Customer Segment Analysis
- Customer and Product Tooltips

Key Insights
- Technology emerged as the top-performing product category, contributing the largest share of total sales.
- A small number of products and customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.
